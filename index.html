Comprehensive Core Banking Features

Microfinance Support

Diverse Loan Products

Deposit Modules

Savings Bank, Current Account, Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit, Daily Deposit, Thrift and Additional Thrift modules.

Membership & Shares

Multi-Branch Banking

Branch Operations
